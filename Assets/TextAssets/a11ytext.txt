Amaze3D Accessibility

Amaze3D is designed to be accessible for blind players. Uncheck Accessibility Mode to disable speech.

Features:

Bump the entrance door of a level to announce objects found on that level.

While in the maze press the letter "I" to list the objects in view.

Press the Tab key to move focus between objects in view.

Use a mouse pointer, or "A" and "D" keys, to turn left and right.

When objects are announced, use the "W" key to walk toward the object.

While announcing, speech is stopped by clicking in the game window.

See How To Play for additional details, press "H".

To open this dialog, press "L".

Press the escape key to dismiss this dialog.
