How To Play

Navigate the game maze using your keyboard:

W - Walk Forward,
S - Step Back,
A - Turn Left,
D - Turn Right,
E - Exit Game,
H - Help (this dialog),
M - Map Toggle,
Q - Quiet Background Music Toggle,
L - Accessibility Details,

Also use the arrow, tab, enter, and spacebar keys.

Move your mouse pointer to look around.

Bump into things to interact with them.

Use the "esc" key to dismiss dialogs like this one. Or, while in the maze, pressing the "S" key to step back will close a dialog.

When exiting the game, the current state is saved. Use Resume Saved in the game menu to continue.
